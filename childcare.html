Child Hygiene Best Practices

Keep your baby clean and healthy with these hygiene tips.

  • 👶 Wash your hands before handling your baby. Make sure baby clothes are changed daily.
  • 🍼 Sterilize baby bottles after every use.
  • 🛁 Bathe your baby 2-3 times a week with warm water.
  • 🧴 Use baby-safe skin products only.
